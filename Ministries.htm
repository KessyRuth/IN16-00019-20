Our Different Ministries

We have five different Ministries

1. THE PRAISE AND WORSHIP MINISTRY

This is the most popular ministry. Many students usually prefer this ministry than the others.
You are welcomed if you want to
join just fill in the forms in the registration page.

2. USHERING MINISTRY

Our ushers are at your service to ensure your comfort is well
taken care of. It is one of the ignored and despised department bt people rarely discover it's benefits.

Our Slogan:#SERVING PEOPLE AS JESUS WOULD.

3. CHOIR MINISTRY

The choir Ministry is different from praise and worship, though both ministries
invole singing and dancing.

4. CREATIVITY MINISTRY

In this ministry you are able to explore and go beyond your limits.
It involves many things that is: Singing, Dancing and Acting among other things.

5. COMPASSION MINISTRY

As students we pass through a lot, and sometimes we enjoy
smooth soft days but sometimes times we have rough times.
The members of compassion ministry are there to help you Financially, Emotionally
and in every other aspect.
You can be part of the ministry or just channel your contributions to our paybill:072234

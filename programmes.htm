History of KSU Christian Union

Christian Union was started in 1995 by Madaam Grace Tombe, After the need of the school to help students grow spiritually.
The members were about 30 at that at the time since students being recruited to the campus were also less.With time the number has grown to about 500, students and we continue expecting more of the students.
Our CU patron is Madaam Rhodah and the deputy Cu patron is Mr.Cephar Omondi.

Our vision is 'to teach' train and equip the body of christ with the word of faith.
